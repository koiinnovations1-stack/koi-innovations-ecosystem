I. What Is Ray Approved

Your Affiliate Storefront

Ray Approved is Rayshun Hopson's curated storefront. It's where you, as a creator, recommend products you genuinely use and believe in. Every recommendation earns you commission. It's not just a link. It's a full commerce experience that feels like you.

III. The Tier System

Where You Earn

Tier 1: Established Platforms
Amazon Influencer · LTK · TikTok Shop Creator · ShopMy. These are the foundation. Easiest to start with. Best for pure commerce.
Tier 2: Creator Networks
Walmart Creator · Impact · ShareASale · Target Circle · Shopify Collabs. Mid-tier programs. Higher commission rates. Wider brand access.
Tier 3: Emerging & Premium
YouTube Shopping (when eligible) · Sponsored content deals · Direct brand partnerships. Highest earnings. Requires audience & influence.

Strategy: Start Tier 1. Build audience. Once you have 5K+ followers, expand to Tier 2. Once you have 50K+ followers, approach brands directly for Tier 3 partnerships.

IV. Getting Started

5 Steps to Launch

Step 1: Apply to Ray Approved
Submit your application at rayapproved.netlify.app/apply. Tell us about your audience. What do you recommend? We approve in 3–5 business days.
Step 2: Connect Your Platforms
Link your Amazon Influencer, LTK, TikTok Shop accounts. Add Instagram & TikTok handles. Your storefront starts building automatically.
Step 3: Add Your Products
Curate your first 10–15 products. Things you actually use and love. Write honest descriptions. Upload photos from your life (not stock images).
Step 4: Create Content
Use our templates. Post on Instagram, TikTok. Tag products. Include the Ray Approved link in bio. Mention earnings potential in video descriptions (transparency builds trust).
Step 5: Track & Optimize
Monitor your dashboard weekly. Which products sell? Which platforms drive most revenue? Double down on what works. Kill what doesn't.
V. Content Strategy

How to Win

The Golden Rules

1. Authenticity first. Recommend things you actually use. Your audience can smell fake. Trust is the only currency that matters.

2. Show the product in action. Don't just link. Show how you use it. Why you love it. What problem it solves for you. This sells.

3. Price transparency. Tell people what it costs. "This [product] is $X and worth every penny because..." Honesty builds authority.

4. Post consistently. 3x weekly minimum. Recommendations buried in your feed don't earn. Featured content does.

5. Disclose affiliates. "I earn commission if you buy through my link" — legally required and builds trust. People respect transparency.

Content Pillars

Tutorial Content: How to use a product. Step-by-step. Solves a problem. High converting.

Unboxing & First Look: Show surprise. Show quality. Show why you love it. Authentic reactions.

Comparison: "I compared X vs Y. Here's why I choose Y." Decision-making content converts.

Haul & Setup: Monthly hauls. Wardrobe refreshes. Room setup. What you bought and why. Relatable.

Behind-the-Scenes: What Ray Approved made you last month. How you make money with recommendations. Transparency sells.
