I. What Is Black Alumni Gala

Ceremony as Sacred

Black Alumni Gala is how we gather as a family — to celebrate achievement, honor legacy, remember the ancestors and builders who got us here, and commit to the future we're building.

The Principle
"We gather in ceremony to remember who we are and who we are becoming."

Annual gathering rooted in excellence, intention, and sacred remembrance. Fashion. Music. Discourse. Celebration. All elements in service of saying: we are here. We matter. We are building something that will outlast us.

II. Ceremonial Dimensions

Elements of Gathering

Fashion & Presence
Everyone dressed with intention. Priauratize suits. Priaura threads. Aesthetic as message. You show up as yourself, elevated.
Discourse & Reflection
Talks. Conversations. The Groove as voice. Speaking what we believe. What we've learned. Where we're headed.
Celebration & Joy
Music. Movement. Gathering as joy. TipsyTAB beverages. Filet Me On culinary. All the ventures serve the gathering.
Legacy & Covenant
Honoring those who came before. Recognizing achievement. Committing to what's next. Memory in service of future.
