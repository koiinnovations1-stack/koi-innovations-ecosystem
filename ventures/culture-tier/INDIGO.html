I. What Is INDIGO

Capital as Stewardship

INDIGO exists to move capital intentionally — into ventures that serve community, into people with vision, into futures that are better because we funded them carefully.

The Principle
"Capital without stewardship destroys. Stewardship of capital builds. We choose to build."

This is not venture capital chasing returns. This is capital as governance tool. Investment shaped by doctrine. Money deployed to strengthen communities, not extract from them.
