I. What Is TipsyTAB

Intentional Discovery

TipsyTAB is a beverage curation platform. We believe discovery should be intentional, guided by knowledge, rooted in craft. Not random. Not flashy. Thoughtful.

The Principle
"Great taste comes from great intention. Discover what moves you."

Whether wine, spirits, craft beer, or non-alcoholic craft beverages, TipsyTAB connects people with drinks that reflect their values and palate. Every recommendation is intentional. Every bottle tells a story.

II. Platform Offerings

Thoughtful Curation

Personal Curation
Understand what you enjoy. Introduce you to drinks you'll love. No algorithms guessing. Real recommendation.
Education
Learn about craft. Understand what makes great beverages. Taste notes. Production stories. The journey behind the drink.
Community Finding
Connect with others who share your taste. Gather around intentional discovery. Build community through beverage.
