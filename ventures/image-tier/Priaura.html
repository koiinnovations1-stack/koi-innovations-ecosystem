I. The Story

Built on Return

Rayshun "Qool Ray" Hopson left his small Mississippi hometown chasing big city opportunities. He discovered the treasure he sought was waiting back home. He returned — not just to his roots, but to his purpose.

Priaura is the expression of that return. A ready-to-wear brand rooted in Mississippi heritage, built on cotton fields once synonymous with oppression, now serving as foundation for liberation. Better threads. Better quality. Clothing that honors both the past and the potential.

The Promise
"Luxury is indulgence, Comfort is a gift. Wear it with intention."

III. Barkley

The Mascot

Barkley is the heart of Priaura identity — a French bulldog embodying loyalty, joy, and unshakeable presence. Crowned with symbols of wealth, loyalty, unity, and power. Barkley represents who wears Priaura: confident, grounded, unapologetically themselves.

Barkley appears across all Priaura collateral, collections, and storytelling. Each appearance carries intentional symbolism. Barkley is not mascot as decoration — Barkley is brand guardian.
